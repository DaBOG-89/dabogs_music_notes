Musical instruments

A musical instrument is a device built or invented to produce musical sounds. Some musical instrument produce sound by stricking, some by plucking of the string, others by blowing in air and some by pressing keys.

Classificaion of Musical Instruments

Musical Instrument have 3 methods of classification;

  • Elementary Classification
  • Ochestra and Non-Ochestra Classification
  • Formal classification

Elementary Classification

The elementary classifications classifies musical instruments into;

  • String Instruments
  • Wood wind Instruments
  • Keyboard Instruments
  • percussion Instruments

Ochestra Classification

The ochestra method classifies musical instruments into;

  • Ochestra Instruments
    • String Instruments
    • Wood wind Instruments
    • Brass Instruments
    • Keyboard Instruments
    • Percusion Instruments
  • Non-Ochestra Instruments
    • String Instruments
    • Wood wind Instruments
    • Keyboard Instruments
    • Percusion Instruments

    • Formal Classification

    The formal method classifies musical instruments into;

    • Idiophones
    • Membranophones
    • Chordophones
    • Aerophones
    • Electrophones

    NB: Those who play musical instruments are generally called instrumentalists.
