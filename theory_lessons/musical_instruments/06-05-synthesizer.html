The Synthesizer

A synthesizer is an electronic musical instrument that creates sound using electric signals instead of physical strings or pipes. It can imitate the sound of traditional instruments or produce completely new and unique sounds.

A synthesizer usually has a keyboard, though some are played using computers, touchpads, or other controllers. It works by generating and shaping electronic waveforms (like sine, square, or sawtooth waves) through components called oscillators, filters, and amplifiers.
