Non Orchestra

Non-orchestral instruments are instruments that are not normally found in a symphony orchestra. They might belong to other musical traditions, bands, or cultures, or they are used mainly in popular, folk, or solo music rather than classical orchestral settings.

1. Popular / Band Instruments

  • Guitar (acoustic, electric, bass guitar)
  • Drum set (kick, snare, hi-hat, toms, cymbals)
  • Saxophone (common in jazz and bands, but rarely used in orchestra)
  • Accordion

2. Traditional / Folk Instruments

  • Talking drum (Gangan, Yoruba drum)
  • Kora (West African harp-lute)
  • Balafon (African xylophone)
  • Sitar (Indian)
  • Tabla (Indian drums)
  • Bagpipes (Scottish)
  • Shamisen (Japanese)

3. Modern / Electronic Instruments

  • Synthesizer / Keyboard (electronic music)
  • Electric violin / electric cello (used outside classical works)
  • Turntables / DJ equipment
