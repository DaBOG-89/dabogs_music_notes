Electrophone Instruments

An electrophone is a type of musical instrument that produces sound using electricity or electronic means.

Unlike idiophones, membranophones, chordophones, and aerophones (which rely on physical vibration of material or air), electrophones generate or amplify sound through electronic circuits.
